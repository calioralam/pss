### 1. **Pendaftaran (Register)**
- **URL**: `http://localhost/uasa/public/register.php`
- **Method**: POST
- **Body**:
  - `username: newuser`
  - `password: newpassword`
- Langkah: Pilih POST → Masukkan URL → Pilih Body (x-www-form-urlencoded) → Masukkan username & password → Klik Send.

### 2. **Login**
- **URL**: `http://localhost/uasa/public/login.php`
- **Method**: POST
- **Body**:
  - `username: newuser`
  - `password: newpassword`
- Langkah: Pilih POST → Masukkan URL login → Pilih Body (x-www-form-urlencoded) → Masukkan username & password → Klik Send.
